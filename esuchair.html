Esu Chair

Esu is a conceptual work chair with an adjustable backrest. It is designed to be used in different working positions with a adjustable backrest than can move up and down, an a backrest that can be turned the desired way. The backrest can be used as backsupport, support for your arm if turned on the side or breastsupport if leaning forward. It can also be used as tailbone support if you adjust it all the way down.

The chair is conceptual and will not be produced.
